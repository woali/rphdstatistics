R packages

  • MixedPoisson (co-author: Michal Trzesiok) - carries out the estimation of the parameters in mixed Poisson models * CRAN version
    • Rdocumentation
  • InsuranceData (co-author: Michal Trzesiok) - insurance datasets, which are often used in claims severity and claims frequency modelling. It helps testing new regression models in those problems, such as GLM, GLMM, HGLM, non-linear mixed models etc.
    • CRAN version
    • Rdocumentation
  • RatingScaleReduction (co-authors: W.W. Koczkodaj, Feng Li) - carries out a new procedure of reducing items in a rating scale called Rating Scale Reduction (RSR)
    • CRAN version
    • Rdocumentation
  • AffluenceIndex (co-author: A. Saczewska-Piotrowska) - Computes the statistical indices of affluence (richness) and constructs bootstrap confidence intervals for these indices. Also computes the Wolfson polarization index.
    • CRAN version
